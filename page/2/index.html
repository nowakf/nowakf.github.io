notes about the city, cosmopolitanism, freedom and utopia 27.04.2017
BACKGROUND: There are a great many texts1 that talk about how, even by simple presence in a city, a sense of freedom is produced.2 This freedom comes in two components: first, the freedom from the basic violence of nature. Second, the freedom from the oppressive family structure, invasive community organization, and violence of traditional farm life.3 The first and second points are perhaps underlying the creative vibrancy of cities.
